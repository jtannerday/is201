My wife Maddie

Maddie and I met in high school and dated for a year before my mission. We wrote each other letters and emails during my mission and continued dating after I got back and began attending college before getting married at the end of 2022. Maddie is the love of my life and my best friend, so glad I get to go through life with someone as incredible as her.

My Family

My family is one of my greatest priorities. My siblings and parents are my best friends and it is a joy whenever we can all get together. I wouldn't be who I am today without them.

Minneapolis, Minnesota My Mission

I served a mission for the church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints from July of 2020 to July of 2022 in the Minnesota Minneapolis Mission. I learned spanish and served mainly in hispanic communities across the Minnesota and Wisconsin. I met a ton of incredible friends and most importantly came to understand and love God and His gospel better than ever before.
